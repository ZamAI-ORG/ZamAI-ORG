# ZamAI Organization

**ZamAI is an AI-first software organization building language technology, educational products, customer-facing AI tools, and practical systems for Pashto-speaking and underserved markets.**

### Our Divisions

#### 1. ZamAI Labs
AI models, datasets, pipelines, and research infrastructure powering our products.

- Pashto datasets & NLP pipelines
- Model training & fine-tuned models

#### 2. ZamAI Products
Customer-facing AI experiences and flagship apps.

- Zeerak (AI assistant)
- ZamAI App
- Keyboard & typing tools
- Code editor

#### 3. ZamAI Education
Mission-driven learning, empowerment, and culturally relevant educational platforms.

- ZamMadrasa
- EduSpeak
- AI Learning App for Afghan Girls
- Study Buddy
- CodeTribe

#### 4. ZamAI Systems
Business and institutional software for digital transformation.

- HR, Finance, Pharmacy, Attendance
- Office automation
- Archive digitization
